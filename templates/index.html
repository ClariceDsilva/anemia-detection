How it works
01
📤 Upload
Upload clear photos of conjunctiva (inner eyelid) or nail beds — the key sites for pallor detection.
02
🔬 Analyze
ML model extracts color histograms, pallor index, saturation, and texture biomarkers from each image.
03
📊 Score
Probabilities across all images are averaged into a final anemia risk score with confidence percentage.
04
🚦 Result
Risk is mapped to Low / Medium / High with a recommendation to consult a physician if elevated.
